# DIGF-2012-001 Low Poly Game Art Winter 2018 **Course name:** Low Poly Game Art **Meetings:** Friday, 3:10pmâ€“6:10pm **Location:** Room 425, Annex Building (MCC) **Course webpage:** Canvas & [http://jiharu.github.io/lowpoly/](http://jiharu.github.io/lowpoly/) **Office hours:** Thursday 2:30 to 3:30pm at 701G, 205 Richmond (hji at faculty.ocadu.ca by appointment) ## Course description Low polygon 3D art occur in real-time applications like digital games that have historically been constrained by the technologies used. However this approach to 3D modelling has become a contemporary art form evidenced across a wide range of contexts from art galleries to game consoles. This studio course surveys a broad range of approaches to building low poly game art exploring 3D production processes and contexts of delivery. Students will produce low poly playable experiences ready for exhibition. ## Course schedule - **Week 1 (Jan 5):** Introduction.
